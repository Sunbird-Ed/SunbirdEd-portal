WELCOME TO Sunbird

Sunbird In-service Teacher Resource App

Teaching is better with a friend! Teach with Sunbird, the official M.A.A app for Sunbird’s school teachers. Sunbird is a one stop solution for digital content aligned to curriculum of Grade 1 to Grade 10. It has resources for classroom teaching as well as self learning. All classroom teaching resources are made by tech savvy teachers of Sunbird and are available in the form of videos, interactive lessons, pdfs, audios and powerpoint presentations. Due to its simple design and layout, content in Sunbird can be easily used by students as well as parents along with teachers.

Sunbird also serves as a channel of communication between M.A.A and teachers. Latest Government Resolutions, notification for teachers are published in a separate News and Updates section. Through a Training Calendar, entire year's training schedule is made available to teachers. Soon, there will be online courses for teachers to hone their existing skills and acquire new ones.
