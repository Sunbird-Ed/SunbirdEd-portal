Browsing for Content

Overview

DIKSHA has a lot of digital content that is related to topics in your textbook.

To look for content that is relevant to you, use the ‘Browse Online’ feature. You can browse for content only when you are connected to the Internet.

Searching for Content

Overview

DIKSHA has a lot of digital content that is related to topics in your textbook.

To search for content that is relevant to you, use the ‘Search’ feature. You can search for content when you are online or offline. You can search for content using the following options:

  • Search Bar
  • Filter Options
  • QR Codes

Using Search Bar

Search Results

Searching for Specific Content

You can bring down the search results by using the various filter options available.

You can use the filter options such as Board, Medium, Class, Subject or Content Types and find specific content files.

For example, you can search for a Biology (Subject), textbook (Content Type), for Class IV, in English (Medium) under CBSE (Board)

Using QR Codes

QR code is the 6 digit code printed on the textbook. You can use this 6 digit code to search and find content linked with the textbook.

Downloading Content

Overview

After you find content, use the ‘Download to My library’ option to download the content files to your library. You can play the downloaded content files at any time, even when you are offline.

You can also copy the downloaded content to your pendrive. All the files that you copy to your pendrive or computer are saved with a .ecar extension. For example, MATHS_01.ecar

My Library

Overview

The ‘My Library’ feature holds all the files that you have downloaded when you are online. You can play the content in your Library when you are online or offline.

You can also copy the downloaded files from your Library to a pendrive or any other external device.

Uploading Content from Pen Drive

Overview

The “Upload” feature allows you to upload content to the DIKSHA desktop application. You can upload DIKSHA content files (.ecar files) that you have copied in your ped drives.

For example, you can download and copy content files to your pen drive when you are online. You can upload these files from your pen drive to the desktop application and play the content files when there is no Internet.

Unsuccessful Upload

Files may not get uploaded successfully if they are corrupted

If you face this problem:
  1. Connect to the Internet
  2. Browse for the content file
  3. Download it to My Library
  4. Copy it to your pen drive
  5. Upload the content file from your pen drive
